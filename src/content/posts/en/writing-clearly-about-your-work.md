A strong personal site usually becomes easier to trust when it feels more structured and less performative.

That often means:

- shorter paragraphs
- clearer section headings
- fewer competing calls to action

Good writing on a site does not need to sound grand. It needs to help the reader move from uncertainty to understanding.
