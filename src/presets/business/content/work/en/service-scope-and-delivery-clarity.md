This sample case is useful when the work was not just specialist output, but also structuring the engagement so the client could understand what was being bought.

## Context

The starting point was a broad request with mixed goals, unclear scope, and too many possible next steps.

## Contribution

The work involved reframing the problem, defining deliverables, and giving the client a simpler route into a practical project plan.

## Outcome

The end result was a clearer delivery path, better expectation-setting, and less ambiguity at the point of commitment.
