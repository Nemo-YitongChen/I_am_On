One of the easiest ways to make a business site feel more trustworthy is to remove unnecessary decision friction.

That often means:

- fewer competing buttons
- more specific CTA labels
- clearer explanations of what happens after contact

Good service pages usually do not need more noise. They need a cleaner route from interest to action.
